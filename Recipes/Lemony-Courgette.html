Lemony Courgettes

This recipe provides instructions to create a healthy meal of spiralised Courgettes and linguine in a lemon juice.

The recipe is quick and easy to make.

Ingredents

  • 2 - Courgettes
  • 150g - Linguine
  • 1 - Lemon
  • Handful of Fresh Mint
  • Handful of Grated Parmasan Cheese

Method

  1. Spiralise the courgettes
  2. In a boiling pot cook the Linguine
