Poached Eggs on Toast

This recipe provides instructions to create a healthy meal of poached egg on toast

The recipe is quick and easy to make.

Ingredents

Serves 2

  • 2 - pieces of bread
  • 1 - Lime
  • 1 - Avocado
  • 2 - eggs

Method

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Scoop out the Avocado (removing the stone)
  3. Squeeze the Lime Juice and mash the Avocado
  4. Spread the Avocado mix onto the toast
  5. In a pan of boiling water stir while adding the egg. Cook the egg for 20 to 30 seconds
  6. Add the poached eggs onto the toast
