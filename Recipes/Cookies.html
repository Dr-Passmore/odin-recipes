Student Cookies

This recipe provides instructions to create cookies.

The recipe is quick and easy to make.

Ingredents

Makes 14 Cookies

  • 75g - Butter
  • 110g - Sugar
  • 135g - Oats
  • 135g - Flour
  • 4 tbsps - Sunflower Seeds
  • 4 tbsps - Sesame Seeds
  • 4 tbsps - Veg Oil
  • 75g - Chocolate Chips
  • 1 - Egg

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees (Fan)
  2. Mix Oats, Flour, Sunflower Seeds, and Sesame Seeds in a bowl
  3. Heat the butter and sugar in a pan. Remove from heat once butter has melted
  4. Add Butter and Sugar Mixture to the bowl.
  5. Add Veg Oil and the egg to the bowl.
  6. Mix all ingredents together.
  7. Add in the Chocolate Chips and seperate into 14 balls
  8. Put on trays and cook for 15 minutes.
